TwitchTOS Quick Tutorial

Quick Intro

Thank you for installing TwitchTOS Ratings. This extension allows you to rate videos on YouTube and approve them/report them for TOS.

Pinning the extension

First of all, I recommend you pin the extension by clicking on the puzzle icon in the top right corner of your browser and clicking the pin button next to the extension.

Logging in to Twitch

Click on the extension icon in the top right, and sign in with your Twitch account. You can also use the button below for now.

Your Profile

If everything went well, you should see your profile when you click on the extension icon now. On the bottom you have your XP bar and on the right you can see your current Twitch badge if you have one unlocked. You get 5XP per vote and leveling unlocks badges that you can use in Twitch chat.

Rating Videos

To rate a video, simply click the FeelsOkayMan or the MonkaTOS after you've logged in. If you're an affiliate your vote counts 5 times, if you're partner your vote counts 10 times. You might notice some creators have been pre-approved: they're guaranteed to be TOS-friendly and won't have a voting system.

Donate?

I spent a lot of time on this extension, if you like what I do and would like to support me in the future, please click the heart in the bottom right to donate. You can unlock this cool donator badge in Twitch chat if you do!
